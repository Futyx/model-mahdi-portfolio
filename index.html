About

Mahdi is a dynamic force in the men's fashion and style industry. Over his four-year career, he has captivated the industry with his exceptional professionalism and unique aesthetic.

Fashion Campaigns

His versatility has made him a go-to choice for international brands like Pepe Jeans, GQ, and Ounass, while his commanding presence has graced major runways including Fashion Week in Dubai and Goa Fashion Week.

Runway Shows

Mahdi's ability to seamlessly blend contemporary style with timeless confidence sets him apart, solidifying his position as a top-tier model.

Global Reach

With a strong presence in key fashion markets like Dubai and India, Mahdi has quickly established a global reach. His experience with international brands and participation in worldwide fashion events make him a versatile model with a broad professional scope.
